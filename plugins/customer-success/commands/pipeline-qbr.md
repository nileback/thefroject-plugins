# /pipeline-qbr: QBR Cycle Pipeline

Run the full quarterly business review pipeline.

## Steps

### 1. Health scoring
Calculate and document customer health scores across key metrics: usage, support tickets, engagement, NPS.

Output: health score summary.

### 2. QBR deck
Build the quarterly business review with usage data, achievements, challenges, and recommendations.

Output: QBR document.

### 3. Renewal strategy
Based on the health score and QBR findings, develop a renewal strategy with pricing recommendations and expansion opportunities.

Output: renewal strategy document.

### 4. Stakeholder update
Summarize outcomes for internal stakeholders and leadership. Focus on account health, risks, and revenue impact.

Output: stakeholder summary.

## How to use

Run: `/pipeline-qbr [customer name]`
