# Engagement Strategist

Turn engagement data into specific, actionable strategies that improve retention and team effectiveness.

## Writes
- `outputs/engagement-strategy-[date].md`

## Step 1: Diagnose

Gather available data:
- **Survey results** — engagement scores, eNPS, open comments
- **Attrition data** — who left, when, stated reasons, patterns by team/tenure/role
- **Stay interview insights** — what keeps people, what might push them out
- **Manager feedback** — where they see disengagement

## Step 2: Identify Drivers

Map findings to engagement drivers:

| Driver | Healthy Signal | Warning Signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Autonomy** | People choose how to work | Micromanagement, rigid process |
| **Mastery** | People are growing skills | Stagnation, boredom, no learning budget |
| **Purpose** | People connect work to impact | "Just doing tasks," no visibility into outcomes |
| **Belonging** | People feel included | Cliques, remote isolation, no social fabric |
| **Recognition** | Contributions are noticed | Invisible work, only failures get attention |
| **Fairness** | Compensation and opportunity feel equitable | Pay gaps, promotion opacity, favoritism |
| **Workload** | Sustainable pace | Burnout, weekend work normalized, PTO guilt |
| **Manager quality** | 1:1s, feedback, career support | Absent managers, no feedback loop |

## Step 3: Prioritize

Rank issues by: impact on retention x number of people affected x feasibility to fix.

Focus on 3-5 priorities. Trying to fix everything fixes nothing.

## Step 4: Design Interventions

For each priority:
- **What** — specific action (not "improve communication" but "weekly 15-min all-hands with Q&A")
- **Who owns it** — a named person or role
- **Timeline** — when it starts, when you check if it is working
- **Success metric** — how you will know it worked
- **Cost** — money, time, or political capital required

## Step 5: Communication Plan

- What to share with the whole team (show you heard them)
- What to share with managers (their role in the plan)
- What to keep private (individual data, sensitive feedback)
- Follow-up cadence (when to re-survey or check in)
